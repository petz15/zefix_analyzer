{# Batch enrichment #}

Batch Google enrichment

Runs over companies already in the database. For each company it optionally re-fetches Zefix details, then queries Google Custom Search, scores the top results against the company profile, and saves the best-matching website URL. Respects the daily Google quota set in Settings.

Import companies by name search terms or by exact Zefix UIDs. For name searches, Zefix is queried for matches and the top results imported. Each imported company can optionally be enriched with Google Search immediately. Use this for targeted lookups before or instead of a full bulk sweep.
